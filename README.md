## National Cyber League (NCL) Reflection
Competing in the National Cyber League was a great exercise in applying my methodologies to a fast paced environment. It was a challenge to efficiently parse through massive datasets, isolate malicious traffic, and pivot strategies while the clock is ticking. The competition made me use lots of tools I didn’t know, so it was necessary to quickly adapt and use my fundamental knowledge to perform well. This experience validated my foundational knowledge and highlighted the areas where I want to continue deepening my technical expertise.

### Key Highlights
* **Network Traffic Analysis:** Effectively analyzed network traffic to trace adversary activity, identify attack vectors, and reconstruct timelines under competition conditions.
* **Cryptography:** Deciphered encoded communications and complex cipher texts by identifying encryption algorithms and utilizing standard cryptanalysis tools to extract hidden flags.
* **Collaboration & Teamwork:** Actively contributed to team-based challenges, practicing effective communication, task delegation, and collective problem-solving to achieve our objectives.
